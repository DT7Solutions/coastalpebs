Solar Pannel

Solar Power System

Solar power systems can be installed on PEBs to harness the sun's energy and convert it into electricity.

Non-Polluting

generate electricity using the power of the sun, which is a renewable and clean source of energy. This means that solar panels do not produce any greenhouse gas emissions, air pollution, or harmful waste products during their operation.

Demand Response

solar panel demand response is an effective way to manage the integration of renewable energy sources like solar into the electrical grid, while also ensuring grid reliability and reducing the overall cost of electricity for consumers.

Load Shifting

Solar panel load shifting refers to the practice of adjusting the timing of energy consumption to align with periods of high solar production, allowing for more efficient use of solar-generated electricity.
