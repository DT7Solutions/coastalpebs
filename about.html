About Our Company

35 Years Of Manufacturing Excellence With Quality At It's Best!

Coastal PEB Solutions was founded by childhood friends Mr Shiva Shankar Rao and Mr Ramesh in 1984. Inspired by Shiva Shankar Rao's father, who had a welding shop, they started Coastal Engineering as a small startup, manually erecting PEB structures. Over time, the company grew and became a leader in PEB. In 2015, with the transition to full automation in PEB manufacturing, the company was renamed Coastal PEB Solutions Pvt Ltd.

Coastal PEB Solutions Pvt Ltd is dedicated to transforming the PEB industry, setting new benchmarks, and making a positive impact on the built environment.We are dedicated to adding new accomplishments to our journey and maintaining our position as a leader in the field of PEB, while always prioritizing the satisfaction and success of our customers.

Coastal PEB Solutions Pvt Ltd is a trusted destination for all your PEB (Pre-Engineered Building) structural needs.We are driven by customer satisfaction and fueled by the faith and support of our valued clients. Our vision is to empower businesses, foster lasting relationships,and offer innovative solutions that establish us as a leader in the industry.
