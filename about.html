Working Plan

PEB Working Process

01

Design and Engineering

involves creating a detailed plan that includes the layout, dimensions, and materials.

02

Fabrication

involves creating the building components, such as the steel frames, roof panels, and wall panels, according to the specifications.

03

Delivery and Installation

The installation team should follow the design plan closely to ensure that the building is erected properly.

04

Finishing and Inspection

This includes adding any finishing touches to the building, such as insulation, cladding, and interior features.
