About Our Company

35 Years Of Manufacturing Excellence With Quality At It's Best!

Coastal PEB Solutions Pvt Ltd is a trusted destination for all your PEB (Pre-Engineered Building) structural needs. With over three decades of experience, we prioritize trust, quality, and timely service. As the first and only PEB manufacturing company in both Guntur and Prakasam districts, we excel in problem-solving and provide high-quality solutions for structural challenges. Coastal PEB Solutions was founded by childhood friends Mr Shiva Shankar Rao and Mr Ramesh in 1984. Inspired by Shiva Shankar Rao's father, who had a welding shop, they started Coastal Engineering as a small startup, manually erecting PEB structures. Over time, the company grew and became a leader in PEB. In 2015, with the transition to full automation in PEB manufacturing, the company was renamed Coastal PEB Solutions Pvt Ltd. Today, Coastal PEB Solutions Pvt Ltd is a game-changer in the PEB industry, offering both conventional systems and PEB structures. We are driven by a constant desire to achieve more and strive to add numerous accomplishments to our portfolio. Trust us to deliver innovative and reliable solutions to meet your structural requirements.
